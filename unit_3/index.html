Honey Hi

Everyone should feel good more often

Honey Hi’s goal is to bring more of these good-feelings to the Echo Park community. We have created a seamless experience that draws on nutrition, natural medicine, and progressive culinary ideas without a sense of fanaticism. This is not a "vegan” or “paleo” place. This is a common place that anyone can come to find food that tastes and feels good.

We offer:

  • Cold pressed juice
  • Inventive smoothies
  • Traditional bone broths
  • Delicious soups
  • Seasonal salads
